  1.     POLLUX Device - Will determine the intensity of excessive light emitted by the households, commercial establishments, and the community. Also, the device will determine a different kind of pollutants present in the lower-level troposphere in the National Capital Region. It may then be used in the provincial regions where in it can identify possible sites that light pollution doesn't exist and can deliver services and give locals job opportunity to be identified as astrotourism sites; a traverse and astrocamp at high altitudes with activities such as star gazing, meteor showers, and auto photography, experiencing fun while learning the cosmos.
  2.     The second project is the development of (a) Light Pollution Map, and the by-products (b) Air Pollution Simulation Map. and (c) Air Pollution Dynamics Map are the products of the data gathering procedure of th Pollux Device. This will serve as a the guide for determining the most light polluted and air polluted site/location in the NCR. Through this, the local government and national goverment may implement laws in the most probable way on how to conserve light and energy and lessen or eradicate the source of air pollution present in their localities.
  3.     The POLLUX Mobilty Device (PMD) will evaluate the identify location/sites of excessive light and may be further classified as an attempt for reducing light energy consumption. The PMD may be utilized indoor and outdoor, Indoor PMD can evaluate the intensity of the light energy ecploited inside an office, building, commercial establishments, and develop ways on how to save light energy consumption. The device is cinsidered as a form of long-term service on the long run.
  4.     The Communicating Space Mobility Logistics (CoSMoLogi) is a website where in the data will be uploaded and maybe used for further researches and education purposes. It will also include suggested guidelines for energy conservation, and endeavors for astronomical stargazing, astrophysics and aerospace technology applications in the Phillippines to entice researches and young students in the field of astronomy.

How it works?

The proposed Center for Astronomy Research and Development will provide for facility to harness knowledge about space science technologies. The Rizal Technological University (RTU) is the pioneering Higher Education Institutions (HEIs) that specializes in the field of Astronomy and Space Science. RTU offers formal degree education in the field of astronomy; BS Astronomy Technology, and MS Astronomy. For more than a decade RTU fosters astronomy education and awareness throughout the country, and were be able to deliver significant researches which were presented locally and internationally, hence making the university the best niche for Astronomy R&D.

Is it good for me?

The initial R&D program of the center would like to address the unrecognized light pollution that our country has been exposed for several years. Loght pollution is the product of waste enerdy being exploited through the consumption of excessive light energy that is being dispersed by the different air pollutants suspended in the low-level troposphere. These light energies are unnecessary to be lightened but were able to lighten because of its excessive light instruments being deployed by the different commercial establishment, household and the community. A little excessive light pollution can save a burning little fossil fuel, and an immerse light pollution can save more fossil fuels from burning, which also generates other types of pollution such as carbon dioxide that generates greenhouse emissions. Green energy may also be wasted, which this green energy may be used for another purpose than to be wasted in a form of light pollution.
